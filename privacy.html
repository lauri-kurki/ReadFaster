Privacy Policy for ReadFaster

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Welcome to ReadFaster (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit https://www.readfaster.app/ (the "Site") and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

As a hobby project, we aim to collect as little data as possible. However, we may collect the following:

  • Personal Data: If you sign up for a newsletter, waitlist, or account, we collect your email address.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information on how the App is accessed and used (e.g., your IP address, browser type, time spent on pages).
  • Cookies: We use basic cookies to keep you logged in or to understand site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality.
  • To notify you about changes to our project or new features.
  • To provide customer support or respond to your inquiries.
  • To monitor usage and improve the user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers to help run the App. These may include:

  • Hosting: Vercel for hosting our website.
  • Analytics: We may use analytics services to track anonymous site traffic.
  • Email: Third-party email services to send updates if you have opted in.

These third parties have access to your data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/HTTPS) to protect your data during transmission. Please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information.

5. Your Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

  • Access/Export: You can request a copy of the data we have about you.
  • Correction: You can ask us to update or fix inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal data at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at infoREMOVETHIS@readfasterSPAM.app.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: infoREMOVETHIS@readfasterSPAM.app
  • Website: https://www.readfaster.app/
